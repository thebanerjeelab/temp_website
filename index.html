Welcome to the Banerjee Lab

Chemical Engineering and Bioengineering lab in the Swanson School of Engineering at the University of Pittsburgh

Our lab is focused on multiple approaches to engineering islets for type 1 and 2 diabetes. We actively conduct research in a variety of fields, including stem cell engineering, organ-on-a-chip, microfluidics, vascularization, hydrogel 3D culture platforms, and bioprinting.
