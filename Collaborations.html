Research Collaborations

Our lab's interdisciplinary nature allows us to reach out to form connections across fields. We are proud to work with these fellow scientists and engineers to tackle difficult research problems.

Thymus Differentiation - Yong Lab, AHN, Institute of Cellular Therapeutics

In this project with Yong Fan, Ph.D, we have developed a novel protocol for differentiattion of iPS cells into thymic-mimetic cells using 3D alginate encapsulation. We provide these cells to the Yong Lab to create a Type 1 Diabetes humanized mouse model.

Lans Taylor, Ph.D.

We work on islet on a chip with his lab. They do the liver, we do the islets.

Kumta, Ph.D.

We have worked with Dr. Kumta on a number of projects in the past and present, including alginate-peptide conjugation for stem cell propogation and alginate bioprinting.

Jay Hoying, Ph.D.

We work with Dr. Hoying on vascularization of islets.
